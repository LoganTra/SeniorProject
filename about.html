Pines Hill Farm

Pines Hill Farm is a small sustainable farm in the White Mountains of New Hampshire. We are family owned and operated. Together we raise beef, pork, chicken and eggs. Click through our products to learn more about how we proudly raise these products. We have a farm store located on our farm open year round where you can purchase our pasture raised cuts and a variety of other local farmers goods. Including maple syrup and other maple products, raw honey and other hive favorites and a variety of vegetables in the warmer months.
